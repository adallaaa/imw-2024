What Are Responses?

GDES-3091 is more than learning how to code, but rather also learning WHY it's important to learn how to code. Throughout the semester we are posed with articles which we are insturcted to read through and give detailed responses to our opinions on them.


Whilst each article has it's own piece to say, they all are interesting as a read on it's own. They talk about the corporatization of the web, and how it grew from these knit hand made websites to copy and pasted generic web design, to different ways in how we should view web, and interaction as a whole.
